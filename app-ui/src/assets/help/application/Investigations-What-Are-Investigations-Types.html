You can have as many investigations types as you like. Initially, you will find 3 examples that you can use as templates or startign points to create your own.

  • Compliance: indicates a violation of a company rule that needs to be reported, investigated and fixed.
  • Improvements: suggests that some inefficiencies discovered during the process mining analysis should be reviewed and the process improved.
  • Reviews: is like an "Ask the expert" type of request.
