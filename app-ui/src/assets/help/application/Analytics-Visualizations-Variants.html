Variants represent ways you executed your processes. Each variant is a sequence of activities that has been followed by one or more case.

Variants can be explored, focusing on

  • Distribution by frequency or performance
  • Top frequency: discover the most frequent paths corresponding to your 80% cases.
  • Bottom performance: discover the worst cases in terms of duration and identify the outliers.

    • Select one or more variants from the table and visualize the corresponding process map.
