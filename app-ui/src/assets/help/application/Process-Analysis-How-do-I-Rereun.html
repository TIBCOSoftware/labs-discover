When an analyis moves to a Not ready state, it means something prevented from running the process mining job successfully.

In such a situation we recommend the following:

  • Read the error message by hovering your mouse around the "Not ready" status column for the analysis. that should give you some hint of the underlying problem.
  • Read the help section on most common mistakes and see whether some solutions are suggested.
  • Fix the underlying problem. Most of the time that could be related to the dataset or mappings.
  • Edit the analysis, and click on Rerun action.
